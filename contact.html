Founded in the early 1970s by a passionate vintner in the picturesque Napa Valley, Chateau La Lumière began as a modest endeavor with a goal to craft exceptional wines that capture the essence of the region. Initially sourcing grapes from local vineyards, the company quickly gained acclaim for its meticulously crafted Cabernet Sauvignon and Chardonnay, leading to a loyal following. Over the decades, the winery expanded its estate and embraced sustainable farming practices, focusing on biodiversity and regenerative viticulture. With multiple award-winning vintages to its name and a reputation for innovation, Chateau La Lumière has evolved into a beacon of quality in the wine industry, while remaining committed to its founding principles of craftsmanship and environmental stewardship.

Get in touch

At Chateau La Lumière, our commitment to creating the best wines is rooted in a passion for excellence and a respect for the land. We believe that the finest wines begin in the vineyard, which is why we employ sustainable farming practices that nurture the environment and enhance grape quality. Our expert viticulturists meticulously monitor each vine, ensuring optimal growing conditions and minimal intervention. In the winery, traditional techniques meet modern innovation, allowing us to express the unique terroir of Napa Valley in every bottle. Our winemakers are dedicated to crafting wines that not only reflect the artistry and science of winemaking but also evoke a sense of place and time. This unwavering dedication to quality and authenticity drives us to push boundaries, continually refining our techniques and exploring new varietals, all in pursuit of creating exceptional wines that delight and inspire.

Velvet Midnigh

"Velvet Midnight" presents a rich tapestry of flavors, featuring luscious dark cherry and blackberry notes intertwined with hints of ripe plum. The wine is accented by subtle spices like clove and cinnamon, providing warmth and depth, while a whisper of dark chocolate adds a luxurious touch. Its velvety tannins provide a smooth mouthfeel, balanced by a vibrant acidity that lingers into a long, sumptuous finish, evoking the allure of a moonlit evening.

Silken Aurora

"Silken Aurora" captivates the palate with a bright medley of crisp green apple and succulent pear, elegantly supported by subtle notes of citrus zest and a touch of honeysuckle. Hints of white peach and melon add a luscious sweetness, while a refreshing minerality and lively acidity provide balance and lift. The finish is smooth and lingering, leaving a delicate impression of floral and herbaceous nuances, reminiscent of a serene dawn.
